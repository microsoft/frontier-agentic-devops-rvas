The experience

How it works.

Challenges are built for two audiences — students do the work, coaches facilitate and verify. Everything is data-driven from a single metadata contract, so the site always reflects what's actually in the repository.

01
Pick a challenge

Browse the catalog. Filter by module, difficulty, or tags. Every card tells you exactly how long it takes and what you need to know.

02
Provision your env

Each challenge creates its own namespaced resources — repos, boards, workflows. Bring an org, run the setup script, and you're ready.

03
Do the work

The student guide walks you through each step. When you're done, the success criteria tell you exactly what "done" means.

04
Coach validates

The coach guide (behind a toggle) has the rubric, verification commands, and facilitation notes. No guesswork on either side.
